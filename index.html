8-bits of ice for Linux and browsers


Welcome to a past where everything was blocky and TV's gave you squared eyes! This is a game for everybody who loves sports games from the 80's and early 90's.

Put on your virtual skates and play the computer or your friends in a fast-paced action-filled game on ice!

The game is written in C++ (and to some extent Lua), using the SDL2 library and emscripten to provide browser support.

There is no preliminary release date, it will be released when it is ready. Follow me on twitter to stay updated.
